World Class Features

BMC FootPrints Service Desk offers world class features that make it easy to administer and use for the small-mid size business and for government organizations.

Service Desk

Enable easy and rapid creation of incident tickets, problems, and service requests

Change Management

Plan changes to reduce service outages, manage approvals, and link change requests to problem and incident records.

Service Catalog

Develop, define, and publish your IT services and create a browsable service catalog for different audiences.

Knowledge Management

Create knowledge articles to share information within your organization, and create different knowledge bases for different users.

Configuration Management Database (CMDB)

Create and manage Configuration Items (CIs) to identify problems and store information. Link CIs to other records such as incidents, problems, and change requests. Track contract and warranty information.

Workflow Automation

Automate business processes with an easy to use GUI, eliminating routine and mundate tasks so you can free up your employees.

Reporting

Easily create custom reports based on your business needs, and create graphs and dashboards to visually and quickly relay information. Automatically schedule reports to run.

Unlimited Portals

Configure the system to work how you want it to work. Design different portals for different users or groups, to ensure access to certain information is only available to those who need it.

Dynamic SQL Linking

Connect to any external system that uses a Microsoft SQL Server or PostgreSQL database source to pull data into Footprints.
